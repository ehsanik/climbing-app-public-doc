ClimbingApp Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

ClimbingApp ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address and password when you create an account
  • Google Account Information: When signing in with Google, we receive your Google account email and basic profile information

Climbing Data

  • Climbing Sessions: Route grades, climbing styles (lead/top rope), route types, number of attempts, notes, and timestamps
  • Fall Practice Logs: Fall location, grade, position, repetitions, comfort levels, and comments
  • Awareness Check-ins: Mental and physical state assessments, goals, barriers, and self-reflection responses
  • Breathing Exercise Data: Usage patterns and preferences

File Access

  • Document Access: We access your device's Documents and Downloads folders only when you choose to import CSV files containing climbing data

Technical Information

  • Device Information: App usage analytics and crash reports through Firebase
  • Authentication Tokens: Secure tokens to maintain your login session

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the app's core climbing tracking functionality
  • Store and sync your climbing data across devices
  • Enable account authentication and security
  • Improve app performance and fix technical issues
  • Provide personalized climbing insights and progress tracking

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services, which employ industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • User-specific data isolation

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: Google Firebase for data storage and authentication
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

  • Access: View your stored climbing data within the app
  • Edit: Modify or update your climbing sessions and profile information
  • Delete: Remove individual climbing sessions or your entire account
  • Export: Export your climbing data (feature may be added in future updates)

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
